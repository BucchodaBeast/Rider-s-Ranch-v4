Getting Here

Directions to Sidvokodvo

Sidvokodvo is in the Manzini Region of Eswatini — approximately 50 km south of Mbabane and 30 km southeast of Manzini city.

From MbabaneHead south on MR3, turn east toward Sidvokodvo — approx 50 km, 45 min
From ManziniHead southeast on MR8 — approx 30 km, 25 min
From South AfricaEnter via Oshoek/Ngwenya border, follow MR3 south — approx 90 min from JHB border
From MozambiqueEnter via Lomahasha, follow MR8 west through Simunye — approx 1.5 hrs
