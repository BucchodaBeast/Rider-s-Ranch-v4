Featured Event

The Swazi Rally

The Swazi Rally is one of the most celebrated cycling events in the Kingdom, drawing riders from across the country and from South Africa and Mozambique.

Routes cut through Eswatini's most breathtaking landscapes — Highveld mountains, Middleveld plains, and the warm Lowveld descent. Multi-day, multi-stage, unforgettable.

Riders Ranch goes as a group. The shared experience bonds club members like nothing else.
